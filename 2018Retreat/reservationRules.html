B- RETREAT ATTENDANCE RULES

  1. Kids Rules:
    1. Parents are required to bring their kids to the activity rooms 5 minutes before adult’s sessions and collect them after sessions.

    3. Parents not to interrupt during kids stay in activity room, a servant will come to you when required.
    4. A register of attendance and leaving time it will be in place for parents to sign.
  2. General Rules:
    1. You are expected to attend on time and participate in all designed activities as per time table.
    2. Breakfast is eaten together in the assigned places and times.
    3. During the Divine Liturgy - Men should stand on the left side of the hall and women on the right.
    4. All women are required to cover their heads with a veil upon entering the hall.
    5. All mobile phones must be turned off.
    6. We kindly ask to please avoid any unnecessary movement, unless calming infants at the back room.
